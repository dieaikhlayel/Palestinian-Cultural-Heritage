The Thobe

The traditional Palestinian dress, often heavily embroidered with regional patterns.

Regional Patterns

Each Palestinian region developed distinct embroidery motifs and color schemes.

Symbolism

Common motifs include cypress trees (strength), flowers (prosperity), and geometric patterns.

Contemporary Adaptations

Traditional tatreez adapted to modern fashion and accessories.
