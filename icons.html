Palestinian Cultural Icons

Discover people, symbols and heritage that shape Palestinian identity.

Cultural Icons

National Icons

Handala

A powerful emblem of Palestinian identity and resistance created by Naji al-Ali.

Key of Return

The symbolic key carried by families representing the right of return.

Kuffiyeh

The traditional scarf that became a symbol of Palestinian identity worldwide.

Palestinian Flag

A national emblem whose colors carry deep historical and cultural meanings.

Influential Figures

Mahmoud Darwish

Poet and voice of Palestinian modern literature and longing.

Fadwa Tuqan

Renowned poet who reflected social realities and personal resilience.

Ghassan Kanafani

Writer and intellectual whose works captured Palestinian experience.

Rim Banna

Singer and cultural preservationist of Palestinian songs and folklore.

Artistic & Heritage Icons

Oud

A classic string instrument central to Palestinian and Levantine music.

Olive Tree

A living symbol of life, continuity and connection to the land.

Arabic Calligraphy

An artistic tradition used in manuscripts, murals and religious art.

Tatreez Thobe

The embroidered dress that tells regional stories through patterns and colors.
